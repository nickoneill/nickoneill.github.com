More Objective-C Literals Coming

Hooray! It's inside baseball for anyone who doesn't use Objective-C on a regular basis but one of those nitpicky issues is finally being fixed in a new clang version: literals for NSNumber, NSArray and NSDictionary!

The syntax looks sane and concise, I'm totally psyched to use it. Also, bonus feature: sane accessors without using objectAtIndex: or objectForKey:!
