12 Point Program for Absolutely, Positively No-Fail Guaranteed Success

Jesse Thorn (I think I've mentioned Put This On before but he can adequately be described as a public radio nerd regardless of if you've heard him before) write about things – methods, outlooks, etc – that you should do to Make Your Thing. They're all great notes about specific people who are successful at doing what they care about.

Being Jesse Thorn must be really fascinating. He's one of these people that is involved random projects from all over. Every time I find a new interesting internet show or podcast there's a good 50/50 chance that Jesse has had a hand in it. He's also had a radio show since he was 19 – the same one, albeit with a slightly different name or content – and that takes some enviable dedication to have done something for eleven years straight. I wonder what kind of inspiration he had growing up to develop such awesome habits.
